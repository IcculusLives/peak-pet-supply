Mountain-Ready Nutrition

From raw frozen and refrigerated foods to freeze-dried, kibble, and soft foods — a wide variety to meet every pet's dietary needs, no matter what age, shape, size or breed.

  • High Protein Kibble — fuel for long hikes and cold winters
  • Freeze-Dried Raw — lightweight nutrition for backpacking trips
  • Wet Food Toppers — add hydration and flavor to every meal

Supplements & Natural Remedies

Curated wellness products for joint health, digestion, immunity, stress relief, and seasonal challenges — including CBD pet products for relief and comfort, especially for senior pets. Clean, safe, and effective — perfect for pets living at altitude.

  • CBD & Calming — natural remedies for anxiety from storms, travel, or altitude changes
  • Joint Support — glucosamine and chondroitin for active joints climbing steep trails
  • Paw Protection — balms and waxes against snow, ice, salt and hot rocks
